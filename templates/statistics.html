Lack of Funding

Sources of funding include credit, venture capital, donations, grants, savings, subsidies, and taxes. The Lack of Funding missed in the Education and the School Sector in each New York's Borough : Brooklyn, Bronx ,Queen, Manhattan,Staten Island. It focuses on causal links among four elements: social exclusion, poverty, exits from poverty and overall processes that generate inequality among social categories. Political programmes to address political interests are required. The LineChart and BarChart describe the Situation .

The Illiteracy

The Illiteracy

Functional illiteracy is reading and writing skills that are inadequate "to manage daily living and employment tasks that require reading skills beyond a basic level". Functional illiteracy is contrasted with illiteracy in the strict sense, meaning the inability to read or write simple sentences in any language. A Lack of Funding will produce the high level illiteracy and consequence of school and education.
